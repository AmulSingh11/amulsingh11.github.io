Time Travel Car: Drive Across Dimensions!
Jump into the driver’s seat of the most powerful time-traveling car ever built! In Time Travel Car, you’re not just racing through roads — you’re racing through history itself. Explore the ancient past, witness futuristic cities, and uncover alternate dimensions as you travel through time to stop the collapse of reality! But beware — the space-time continuum is unstable. Fractures appear along your path, and only the most skilled time drivers can dodge them in time.

🚗 Game Features

  • 🌍 Travel Through Time – Race across multiple historical eras — from ancient civilizations to cyberpunk futures. Each era brings its own challenges, environments, and obstacles.
  • 🌀 Dimensional Travel – Use your car’s special abilities to enter alternate realities and hidden worlds. Not all dimensions follow the same rules!
  • 💥 Superpowers & Upgrades – Unlock powerful car abilities like:
    - Giant Mode – Crush obstacles by increasing your size
    - Shrink Mode – Fit through narrow rifts in time
    - Jump Ramp – Launch yourself over chaos
    - Speed Zone – Create bursts of time-speed to boost through danger
    - More powers coming soon!
  • ⚠️ Dodge Space-Time Fractures – Mysterious cracks in space appear across the roads — dodge them before they pull you into the void!
  • 🏆 Achievements & Leaderboards – Compete globally! Beat your high scores and unlock rare achievements. Show who’s the ultimate time rider.
  • 🎮 Simple Controls, Deep Gameplay – Easy to pick up, but hard to master. Enjoy smooth gameplay whether you’re a casual player or a speedrunner.
